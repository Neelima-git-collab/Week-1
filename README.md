**Problem Statement:**
To analyze the 2025 Electric Vehicles (EV) dataset to understand the specifications, performance factors, and pricing relationships that influence the EV market.

**Project Description:**
This project aims to perform data analysis on electric vehicle specifications, including range, battery capacity, price, and manufacturer details. The Week-1 goal is to clean and understand the dataset to prepare it for deeper insights and AI-based predictions in upcoming weeks.

**End Users:**

* EV manufacturers and R&D teams
* Consumers comparing EVs
* Environmental and transport policy researchers
* Automotive data analysts

**Technologies Used:**

* Python
* Pandas, NumPy (data handling)
* Matplotlib, Seaborn (data visualization)
* Jupyter Notebook

**Week-1 Findings:**

* Dataset loaded and explored successfully
* Missing and duplicate records handled
* Summary statistics and correlations identified
* Range, price, and battery capacity visualized
* Dataset saved in a cleaned format for Week-2 modeling
